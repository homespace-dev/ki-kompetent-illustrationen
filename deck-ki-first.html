How AI creates value

Three impact levels – from efficiency to business model.

  • New ways of working & collaboration

Creating New Business Models

New products, services & revenue streams beyond today's business.
  • Data-driven services & platforms
  • Outcome- & service-based models
  • Personalized, scalable customer solutions

Success = basis + leadership + organization

Technology is the basis – leadership & organization decide.

Leadership
Set direction & decide where AI creates value.
  • Understand business priorities & AI potential
  • Decide where to apply AI (prioritization)
  • Drive alignment & act as a role model
Technology
The scalable, secure foundation.
  • Data quality & availability
  • Platforms & infrastructure
  • AI tools, models & security
Organization
Embed AI effectively across the organization.
  • Clear roles, responsibilities & governance
  • Employee enablement & training
  • Establish "AI Value" thinking
11
Finding use cases

How AI use cases emerge across impact levels.

Transforming Core Processes

Structured · Top-down
  • Start from strategic priorities & process pain points
  • Analyze core processes & performance gaps
  • Derive use cases from clearly defined problems

Empowering Employees

Bottom-up · Usage-driven
  • Enable employees with AI tools (e.g. copilots)
  • Encourage experimentation in daily work
  • Identify recurring use cases from real usage

Creating New Business Models

Exploratory · Innovation-driven
  • Start from customer needs & untapped opportunities
  • Explore new value propositions & models
  • Use innovation frameworks (e.g. JTBD)

Part 3 · How to get started

Five steps to get started.

1LEADERSHIP

Start with yourself

  • Use AI yourself – build your own agent
  • Understand capabilities through usage
  • Gain first-hand experience
2URGENT

  • AI is already being used – shape it
  • Provide the right tools quickly
  • Prevent shadow AI
3FOUNDATION

Build the foundation

  • Assess skills & roles
  • Identify capability gaps
  • Build structure & responsibilities
4PLATFORM

Build a scalable platform

  • Data, tools & infrastructure
  • Secure, integrated environment
  • Enable reuse & scalability
5USE CASES

Prioritize use cases

  • Focus on high impact
  • Prioritize a few use cases
  • Start with MVPs & scale
